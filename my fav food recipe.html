FOOD RECIPE

FLUFFY PANCAKES

Ingredients

  1. ¾ cup milk
  2. 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  3. 1 cup all-purpose flour
  4. 2 tablespoons white sugar
  5. 1 teaspoon baking powder
  6. ½ teaspoon baking soda
  7. ½ teaspoon salt
  8. 1 egg
  9. 2 tablespoons butter

    10. Method
  10. Combine milk and vinegar in a medium bowl and set aside for 5 minutes to make sour milk.
  11. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large mixing bowl.
  12. Whisk egg and butter into soured milk.
  13. Pour flour mixture into milk mixture and whisk until lumps are gone.
  14. Heat a large skillet over medium heat, and coat it with cooking spray.
  15. Pour 1/4 cupfuls of batter onto the skillet, and cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, about 2 to 3 minutes.
  16. Flip with a spatula, and cook until browned on the other side.
  17. Repeat with remaining batter.
